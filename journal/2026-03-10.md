### Key Insights

1. **All 5 cities profitable** — even Miami squeaked out +$22 (was -$76 in 1yr run)
2. **Temperature variability drives edge** — Chicago (highest) dominates, Miami (lowest) is marginal
3. **Low win rate is expected** — strategy takes many small-edge NO positions on tail brackets; losses are small, wins pay out significantly
4. **1.1% max drawdown** — conservative 0.1 fractional Kelly keeps risk very low
5. **Brier 0.2571 vs 0.20 target** — the proxy model (persistence+climatology) scores near climatological baseline (~0.25). The live 4-model ensemble with 143 NWP members should score well below this.
